# Style Analysis Matrix

Use this matrix when extracting patterns from high-liked Xiaohongshu images.

## Engagement Context

- Keyword or topic
- Product category
- Note title or hook
- Likes, saves, comments, shares if available
- Image order in the note
- Repeated visual pattern across winners

## Visual Dimensions

| Dimension | What to Observe | Prompt Translation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Camera angle | Top-down, 45-degree, eye-level, low angle, mirror selfie, over-shoulder, macro close-up | "shot from a 45-degree angle", "top-down flat lay", "eye-level phone photo" |
| Distance | Macro detail, close-up, medium product scene, room context | "tight crop with product filling 40% of frame", "medium lifestyle scene" |
| Composition | Centered, rule of thirds, diagonal layout, grid, messy-real, negative space, hand entering frame | "product on lower-right third with props diagonally arranged" |
| Product role | Hero object, ingredient proof, in-use object, background proof, before/after result | "product as the hero, clearly readable silhouette" |
| Scene | Desk, bathroom, vanity, cafe table, bedroom, kitchen, gym bag, travel pouch, store shelf | "real apartment vanity scene with daily-use objects" |
| Props | Ingredients, tools, fabric, notebook, cup, flowers, receipt, bag contents, packaging inserts | "supporting props that imply routine and credibility" |
| Lighting | Window light, flash, softbox, warm lamp, daylight shadow, glossy highlight | "soft natural window light from left, gentle shadows" |
| Color | Cream neutrals, fresh green, clean white, candy color, muted gray, warm wood, high contrast | "warm white and sage green palette with one accent color" |
| Texture | Linen, wood, ceramic, glass, metal, paper, skin, foam, condensation, powder | "tactile linen background, ceramic dish, glossy product surface" |
| Human presence | Hand holding, face crop, body part, mirror reflection, no human | "one natural hand entering frame, no full face" |
| Text overlay | Handwritten labels, app-style notes, stickers, arrows, comparison labels, minimal text | "small handwritten Chinese annotation labels, clean and unobtrusive" |
| Proof cue | Ingredient, result, comparison, routine, quantity, receipt, empties, review snippet | "subtle proof elements: opened package, used applicator, before/after card" |
| Mood | Trustworthy, cozy, premium, energetic, clinical, playful, handmade, expert | "cozy but clean recommendation tone" |

## Prompt Checklist

Before finalizing prompts, ensure each one names:

- Product category and package shape
- Desired aspect ratio, usually 3:4, 4:5, or 1:1 for Xiaohongshu
- Camera angle and crop
- Scene and props
- Lighting direction and softness
- Color palette
- Human/action presence if any
- Text overlay policy
- What must stay accurate
- What to avoid
